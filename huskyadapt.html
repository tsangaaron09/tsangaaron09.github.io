(5/21/21)When I started with this team in 2019, I initially joined because I wanted to gain experience with coding with a team. We were not able to make tangible progress because various members were not able to commit to the project. In the same year, we went through changes with the student groupsâ€™ leadership. I saw this as an opportunity to step up. After we welcomed the new members this year, I gathered the developers onto a call and set up ground rules for commitment and the workflow of this application. I adopted a way of agile/scrum management, incorporating weekly sprints for subtasks within our quarterly goals and scheduled weekly check-ins with the team. After almost 13 weeks of leading the team, we are nearing a finished front-end, both the iOS application and web application, and a database built in with the backend. I am excited to see where this progress goes next, and ultimately, seeing it being used by the hospital!
