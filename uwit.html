<Reflection />

(6/1/21) In September 2020, I had the opportunity to start work with the UW Accessibility Team to help make UW software more accessible to all individuals. When we think about user cases, we rarely think about those that have physical or mental disabilities that may hinder their use of the applications and software. Whether it is a blind individual with visual impairments trying to enter a Zoom meeting or a keyboard user creating a calendar invite, it would be unfair to this demographic of users to not be able to experience the same benefits of a user that do not have disabilities. Fortunately, tech companies are slowly realizing the importance of accessibility and are actively engaged with our team to fix accessibility issues within their products. To date, I am leading 3 major collaborations and co-leading other collaborations with my co-workers. Through this year, I have participated in fixing 75+ issues with many more in progress.


If you would like to learn more about what we do here at the UW Accessibility Team, please do not hesitate to reach out!
